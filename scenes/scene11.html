10 June 2026.
Zamalek.
Our second date.
By then...
we were already getting closer.
Without even realizing it.
I don't remember
what we talked about.
Because...
I barely spoke.
I just kept looking at you.
Quietly trying...
to remember
every little detail.
The way you listened.
The small pauses
between your smiles.
I was afraid...
the day would end
before I had seen enough of you.
Funny...
how a few quiet hours
can stay with you forever.
Back then...
it was simply
another beautiful day.
I had no idea...
how much our story
was about to change.
